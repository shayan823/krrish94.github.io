About me

I am a postdoctoral associate at MIT CSAIL with Josh Tenenbaum and Antonio Torralba. I earned my PhD ( see thesis) at Mila and the Robotics and Embodied AI Lab, advised by Liam Paull.

My research centers around building world models — representations that encapsulate various real-world phenomemon that are essential to designing intelligent systems. In doing so, my research spans several areas including robotics, computer vision, computer graphics, and intertwines our understanding of the world with deep learning.

My work has been recognized with PhD fellowship awards from NVIDIA and Google, and a best-paper award from IEEE RAL.
